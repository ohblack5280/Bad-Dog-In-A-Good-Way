About Poultrygeist"

You might have heard of Poultrygeist. If so, you know it's a great place to find all of your fun and spooky chickeny halloween needs. But did you know inside of Poultrygeist, thier is also a sinsister but delicious place to eat called "Night of the Chicken Dead". This one-of-its-kind, poultry store contains scary chicken treasures for everyone!

Originally opened in 1666, Poultrygeist wanted to have a place where fans of Halloween and chicken could come to have a great time. So, the combination of haunting spirits and fried chicken came to be. We want ghoul and goblin lovers of all ages to also enjoy some ghosty wings.
